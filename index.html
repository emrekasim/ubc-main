Standby Letter of Credit

UBC assure providing a standby letter of credit through our partners to all customers without any difficulties. The Letter of Credit is a guarantee provided by a bank or financial institution where the payee is paid on behalf of the customer when the applicant defaults. This financial product is the most preferred and flexible tool for secure payments.

In the last decades, Standby Letter of Credit has become the most common financial instrument for the purpose of international trade. We assure that every customer will not have any difficulties or hassle. Our financial experts will provide all the necessary guidance.
